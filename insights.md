# 📊 Insights & Recommendations

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🚕 Trip Purpose & Duration

* Meetings have the longest average trip duration
* Customer visits follow as the next longest trip purpose
* Business trips dominate total trips with 93.33%

---

### 🛣️ Trip Distance Analysis

* Meetings and customer visits account for the highest miles traveled
* Temporary site and errand trips are shorter distances

---

### 🔄 Long Trips (>60 minutes)

* Significant number of long trips occur between Cary and Winston Salem
* Other notable origins include Wayne Ridge and Edgehill Farms

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### ⏳ Improve Long Trip Efficiency

* Explore routing optimizations between key locations like Cary and Winston Salem
* Consider alternative transportation methods or scheduling to reduce long trip times

---

### 📈 Enhance Business Trip Management

* Focus on optimizing meeting-related trips to reduce time and cost
* Encourage use of personal trips where possible to reduce operational load

---

### 🚀 General Improvements

* Monitor trip categories and durations continuously for emerging patterns
* Use data insights for strategic planning of driver allocation and route management

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis shows a strong dominance of business-related trips with long durations, especially meetings and customer visits, indicating opportunities for operational improvements and cost savings.
